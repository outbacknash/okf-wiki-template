# Spaced Repetition

Recall is strongest when reviews happen just before you would forget. Spacing
reviews at increasing intervals beats massed practice ("cramming").

Relevant to my [Learn Spanish](../projects/learn-spanish.md) project — use an SRS
app for vocabulary rather than re-reading lists.
