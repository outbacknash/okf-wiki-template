# Admins dismiss the tutorial overlay

> "I'll just click around and figure it out — I can come back to this later."
> — P3

6 of 8 participants closed the first-run tutorial overlay within ~3 seconds,
before reading it. Observed in the
[Onboarding Usability Test (Q2)](../studies/onboarding-q2.md).

Supports the insight: [New admins skip the setup
tutorial](../insights/admins-skip-tutorial.md).
