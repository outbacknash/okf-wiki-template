# New admins skip the setup tutorial

[New Admin](../personas/new-admin.md) users dismiss the up-front tutorial because
they are motivated to complete a real task, not to read instructions first.

## Evidence
- [Admins dismiss the tutorial overlay](../nuggets/skip-tutorial-overlay.md)
  (6 of 8 participants).

## So what
Leads to: [Defer the tutorial until first real
task](../recommendations/defer-tutorial.md).
