# New Admin

A first-time admin who just signed up and is setting up the workspace for their
team. Goal-driven and time-pressured; wants to get the team working, not to learn
the product in the abstract.

Referenced by the [Onboarding Usability Test (Q2)](../studies/onboarding-q2.md)
and the insight [New admins skip the setup
tutorial](../insights/admins-skip-tutorial.md).
